HOW DOES THIS WORK?

When you enter a photograph in a SNAPATHON weekly competition you can win points in two ways. Firstly, if your image places 1st, 2nd or 3rd by getting the most votes, you receive 7, 5 or 3 points. Secondly, if you vote for an image that places 1st, 2nd or 3rd, you also receive points. Points and awards are calculated on Sunday evening.

3rd Place Image: 3
